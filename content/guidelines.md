# Community Guidelines
Eyebrow: COMMUNITY
Intro: The Central Guild works best when Adventurers treat shared work, proof, and people with care.

## Be Honest
Submit accurate profile details, quest applications, and completion reports. Do not claim work you did not complete.

## Respect Quest Owners
Read the quest details, communicate clearly, meet agreed expectations, and raise blockers early.

## Keep The Tavern Useful
Community spaces should support collaboration, learning, and accountability. Avoid harassment, spam, impersonation, and unsafe requests.

## Protect The Guild Record
Report suspicious quests, duplicate profiles, false claims, or content that makes the platform less trustworthy.
