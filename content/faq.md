# FAQ
Eyebrow: HELP
Intro: Common questions for new adventurers, quest owners, and community members.

## Who Can Join
Anyone who wants to build skills through real contribution can create an adventurer profile and begin the verification flow.

## What Counts As A Quest
A Quest is a verified project, task, challenge, initiative, or opportunity with a clear contribution outcome. It can be remote, local, short, flexible, paid, volunteer, or recognition-based.

## What Is A Guild ID
A Guild ID is a public identity and participation record. It helps show completed Quests, verified skills, achievements, and growth over time.

## How Verification Works
Quest submissions are reviewed by guild staff or quest owners. Approved work can appear on the adventurer profile and contribute to rank, achievements, and reputation.

## What If A Quest Has No Money Reward
Zero-value rewards are shown as Volunteer, Recognition, and XP so the contribution intent is clear.
