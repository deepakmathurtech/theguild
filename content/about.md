# The Central Guild
Eyebrow: ABOUT
Intro: The Central Guild is a community-driven platform designed to turn learning, contribution, and personal growth into an interactive journey.

## Hero
Build skills. Complete quests. Grow together.

## What Is Guild
Instead of focusing only on resumes, marks, or certificates, Guild encourages people to build real experience through participation. Members join as Adventurers and grow by completing Quests: real-world tasks, projects, initiatives, and opportunities created by the community.

## How Guild Works
Every contribution goes through verification to maintain quality and trust. Completed work becomes part of a member's public profile, creating a living record of skills, experience, and achievements over time.

## Verification Process
Profiles and quest reports can move through Submitted, Under Review, Approved, and Rejected states. Verified work is added to the Adventurer dossier and can support future Ranks, achievements, and public trust.

## Departments
Guild combines the motivation and progression of games with the impact and structure of real communities. Departments organize contribution paths across creation, technology, research, field work, operations, and community activity.

## Ranks
Ranks are progress levels that reflect contribution and growth. They help show how an Adventurer has moved through the journey from consuming to creating, contributing, and leading.

## FAQ
Inside Guild, Adventurers are members building skills and contributing. Quests are verified projects, tasks, challenges, and opportunities. Guild ID is a public identity and record of participation. Community is where people connect, collaborate, and grow.

## Contact
Guild is not built to replace professional platforms. It is built to create the journey before them. For partnerships, corrections, verification questions, moderation requests, or social updates, use the links on this page or contact the guild staff through the official community channel.
