# Privacy
Eyebrow: LEGAL
Intro: The Guild stores only the information needed to run accounts, profiles, quests, approvals, and public adventurer records.

## Information Collected
Registration may store your name, email, contact details, city, specialization, experience, portfolio link, Guild ID, reputation, and quest activity.

## Public Profile Data
Approved adventurer pages can show public-facing details such as name, Guild ID, rank, city, specialization, tagline, portfolio, reputation, verified skills, achievements, and completed quests.

## Protected Records
Private registration records are restricted by Firebase rules. Staff access is used for review, approval, moderation, and system administration.

## Data Care
Keep personal information minimal and relevant. Contact guild staff if profile details need correction or removal from public display.
